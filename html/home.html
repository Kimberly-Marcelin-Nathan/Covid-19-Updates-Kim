WHAT ARE THE MAIN SYMPTOMS?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It can cause symptoms ranging from mild to very severe whilst some of them are listed here

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath

THINGS NOT TO DO DURING COVID

If you're ill with COVID-19 , you can help prevent the spread of the COVID-19 virus also prevent yourself from falling sick again by following these measures!

  • Do Not Share Eatables or Things
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Come in Contact with Sick People

THINGS TO DO DURING COVID

There are many ways your actions can help protect you, your household, and your community from severe illness from COVID-19 follow these during covid!

  • Wash Your Hands For 20 sec
  • Wear a Mask if Available
  • Seek Medical Care Regularly
